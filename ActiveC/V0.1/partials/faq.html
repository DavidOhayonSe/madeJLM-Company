I can't send mail to a student. Why?

If clicking a student's mail doesn't do anything, you probably don't have an email client set up.
For Gmail accounts :
Step 1: Click the Chrome menu in the top right corner of your browser, and choose Settings.
Step 2: Click the Show Advanced Settings link at the bottom of the screen.
Then click the Content Settings button under the Privacy header.
Step 3: In the pop-up window, scroll down to the Handlers section and click the Manage Handlers button.
Then select Gmail from the mailto dropdown, click Done, and, well, you're done!

For Outlook :
Step 1: On the Tools menu in Office Outlook, click Options, and then click the Other tab.
Step 2: Under General, select the Make Outlook the default program for E-mail, Contacts, and Calendar check box.
