What is the ActiveC site?

This site is a new way to find better candidates to fill in a job position in your company.
We offer a simple system in which the students register and fill their skills and career history,
We present to you the highest matching students according to your necessary skills for the job position.

How do i register?

If you are a Jerusalem based company and you would like to use our awesome services
Pleese use one of the many options in the Contact Us page, and ask to be registred.
Made in JLM Organization will be happy to provide you our services for FREE :)
