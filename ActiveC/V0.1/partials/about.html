Welcome to MadeinJlm

MadeinJLM is a community-based non for profit organization, established in 2012 by Hanan Brand, Roy Munin and Uriel Shuraki. Our main goal is connecting, growing and empowering Jerusalem's startup and tech community.

With our partners and volunteers, we make sure that no one is left alone, and enable the thousands of entrepreneurs and more than 500 startups innovating in the city, the opportunity to contribute, connect, participate in events, find jobs, partners and mentors, and become an integral part of one of the most connected and most supportive tech communities in the world, a city that has been innovating for the last 3000 years.
